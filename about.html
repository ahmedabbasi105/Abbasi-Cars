Our History

Abbasi Renta Car was founded in 2001 with a mission to provide reliable and affordable car rental services. Starting with just a small fleet of vehicles, we have grown over the years to become one of the leading car rental services in the region. Our commitment to quality service, customer satisfaction, and innovation has driven our expansion and success. We are proud to offer a diverse range of vehicles to meet the needs of our customers, from compact cars for city commutes to luxury sedans for business trips and spacious SUVs for family adventures. Over the years, we have continually upgraded our fleet, invested in cutting-edge technology, and trained our staff to ensure the best possible rental experience. Our dedication to excellence has earned us numerous awards and a loyal customer base. As we look to the future, we remain committed to providing outstanding service and continuing to lead the industry with innovative solutions and superior customer care.

Our Mission

Our mission at Abbasi Renta Car is to deliver exceptional car rental experiences by providing high-quality vehicles, outstanding customer service, and great value. We aim to make the process of renting a car as easy and convenient as possible, whether for a short trip across town or an extended journey across the country. We are dedicated to maintaining a modern and diverse fleet to meet the unique needs of every customer and ensuring that every interaction with our company leaves our customers satisfied and confident in their choice. Our mission is to continuously improve our services and innovate in the car rental industry to exceed our customers' expectations and foster long-lasting relationships.
